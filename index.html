Welcome to DoughIO.

This easy to use cost splitting service is currently under development. Designed to settle bills without hassle, be it with your friends, flat mates or significant other.

To give some heads up, here's what we think:

  • Forget about login, signup or download
  • Non intrusive, no ads
  • Open to a fault

There are several really great services for splitting costs out there but we just wanted something a little simpler for all the times when you don't want to have to tell all your friends to sign up here or download that. A service where a URL is all you need, a one stop shop micro service.

Please do contribute, add requests or issues!
